Research Fields:
Mathematical modeling · Multi-scale blood circulation · Wave propagation in porous media

My research focuses on mathematical modeling and numerical analysis of multi-scale systems in biomechanics and geophysics, including whole-body blood circulation, wave-induced fluid flow in porous media, and coupled multi-physics problems.

Research Interests

  • Multi-scale modeling of whole-body blood circulation and autoregulation
  • Wave propagation in porous and fractured media (Biot / BISQ / dual-porosity models)
  • Numerical analysis and high-performance computing for coupled PDE–ODE systems
  • Data assimilation and parameter estimation in physiological systems
